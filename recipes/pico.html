Rockfish Pico De Gallo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe roma tomatoes, diced small
  • 1/2 white onion, diced small
  • 1 serrano chili, sliced thin or diced
  • 1" knob of ginger, peeled and microplaned
  • 1 garlic clove, microplaned
  • About 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tin of Tiny Fish Co. Rockfish in sweet soy sauce
  • salt
  • Lime juice

How to make

  1. Combine the diced tomatoes, onion and serrano chili in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add in the garlic, ginger and cilantro.
  3. Flake the rockfish in the tin and then pour the entire contents into the mixing bowl..
  4. Mix well, then season as desired with salt and lime juice.
